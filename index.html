John Ramspacher, aka Johnny Bongos, started playing the smaller macho drums in high school. Since then, with the addition of large hembra drums and other instruments, he has become one of the northeast's premier bongoseros. In addition to these solos, you can catch him playing with several musicians, including his good friend, singer, songwriter mark douglas berardo. Songs are requests played during the COVID-19 Pandemic.
